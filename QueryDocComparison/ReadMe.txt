generateDocWordEmbedding.py-

Generates average vector embedding [1X300] for each txt file and stores the result in CSVs corresponding to respective folders. {I tested on just a small dataset AI 482 papers}


distanceMeasure.py-

Computes various distance measures (reading data from CSVs) to find top 'k=10' most similar documents to the queried one. {Results stored in CSVs}
